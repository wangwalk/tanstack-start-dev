## Newsletter Subscriptions

You can now collect newsletter subscribers directly from your app. Stockholm ships with two provider options out of the box:

- **Resend Audiences** — manage subscribers in your existing Resend account
- **Beehiiv** — sync subscribers to your Beehiiv publication

### Auto-subscribe on sign-up

Set `NEWSLETTER_AUTO_SUBSCRIBE=true` in your environment to automatically add new users to your mailing list when they create an account.

### Subscribe endpoint

A `/api/newsletter/subscribe` endpoint is available for custom subscribe forms. It validates the email, checks for duplicates, and forwards to your configured provider.
